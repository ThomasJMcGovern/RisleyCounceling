CRISIS SUPPORT

If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, please reach out for immediate help:

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

988

24/7 crisis support and suicide prevention services

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741

Free 24/7 support via text message

Los Angeles County DMH

(800) 854-7771

24/7 mental health crisis line for LA County residents
