ABOUT ME

I have varied experience working with clients who suffer from addiction and co-occurring disorders, as well as clients who are recovering from and managing severe mental health issues. My mission is to help you find your authenticity and sense of self in the face of life stressors and transitions.

If you are navigating sobriety or other major life transitions and finding it hard to cope with your new "normal," you are not alone. Healing and growth are often painful experiences. Reach out to find compassion, support, and understanding as we work to help get you where you were always going- towards a loving and supportive internal experience.

OUR APPROACH

INSIGHT-ORIENTED
SOMATIC-BASED
RELATIONAL

We bring a compassionate and experiential approach that invites you to be honest, trustworthy, and loving toward yourself and others. Combining insight about how your lived experiences affect current patterns in your life utilizing the model with somatic based therapy approaches such as EMDR, we harness the connection between your mind, body, and authentic self to process your experiences and emotions deeply so that you can heal and live the life you deserve.
