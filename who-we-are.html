WHO WE ARE

WE ARE A TEAM OF LICENSED THERAPISTS WHO OFFER HIGH-QUALITY THERAPY TO RESIDENTS IN LOS ANGELES FOR INDIVIDUALS, COUPLES, AND FAMILIES

At Risley Leskos Therapy, we believe in creating a safe and nurturing environment where clients can explore their inner world, heal from past experiences, and develop meaningful relationships with themselves and others. Our approach combines insight-oriented therapy with somatic-based practices, allowing for deep emotional processing and lasting change.

Founded by Risley Leskos, LMFT, our practice has grown into a collaborative team of skilled therapists who share a commitment to providing exceptional care. Risley brings over a decade of experience in mental health, specializing in relationship dynamics, trauma recovery, and cultural identity. She holds a Master's degree in Clinical Psychology and is licensed by the California Board of Behavioral Sciences.

Our therapists are carefully selected for their expertise, empathy, and dedication to ongoing professional development. We maintain a strong commitment to cultural competence and creating an inclusive space for all clients.
