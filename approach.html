HEALING MIND, BODY & SPIRIT

My therapeutic approach integrates evidence-based practices with a deep understanding of the interconnection between mind, body, and spirit. By combining traditional psychotherapy with somatic and mindfulness-based interventions, I help clients achieve lasting transformation and authentic healing.

Psychodynamic Therapy

Understanding Your Inner World

Psychodynamic therapy explores how past experiences shape current behaviors and relationships. By bringing unconscious patterns to awareness, we can understand and transform the ways you relate to yourself and others.

  • Uncover unconscious patterns and defenses
  • Explore early relationships and attachment
  • Develop insight into recurring themes
  • Transform self-limiting beliefs
  • Deepen self-understanding and acceptance
◉

EMDR Therapy

Reprocessing Trauma & Distressing Memories

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) is a powerful, evidence-based treatment for trauma and PTSD. This approach helps process disturbing memories and beliefs, reducing their emotional charge and allowing for adaptive resolution.

  • Process traumatic memories safely
  • Reduce emotional distress and triggers
  • Transform negative beliefs about self
  • Enhance emotional regulation
  • Accelerate healing and recovery
☼

Somatic Experiencing

Healing Through the Body

Somatic therapy recognizes that trauma is stored in the body. By developing awareness of bodily sensations and learning to regulate the nervous system, we can release trapped energy and restore a sense of safety and vitality.

  • Release stored trauma from the body
  • Regulate the nervous system
  • Develop body awareness and presence
  • Restore natural resilience
  • Integrate mind-body connection
◑

Mindfulness-Based Interventions

Cultivating Present-Moment Awareness

Mindfulness practices help develop non-judgmental awareness of thoughts, feelings, and sensations. This approach reduces reactivity, enhances emotional regulation, and fosters a deeper connection to your authentic self.

  • Reduce anxiety and stress
  • Enhance emotional regulation
  • Develop self-compassion
  • Improve focus and clarity
  • Cultivate inner peace and acceptance
