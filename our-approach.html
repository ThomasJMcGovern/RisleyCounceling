Our therapeutic approach is tailored to meet you where you are while guiding you toward where you want to be. We integrate various evidence-based modalities with deep empathy and understanding, creating a unique therapeutic experience that addresses your specific needs and goals. Through our collaborative approach, we help you develop insights, build practical skills, and create lasting positive change.

RESTORATION THERAPY

Restoration Therapy is a powerful approach that helps individuals and couples identify and break free from negative patterns in their relationships and lives. By understanding your core patterns and emotional triggers, you can develop new ways of responding and create healthier relationships.

YOU MAY BENEFIT FROM THIS APPROACH IF:

  • You feel stuck in negative relationship patterns
  • You struggle with emotional regulation
  • You want to improve communication
  • You're dealing with family of origin issues
  • You want to break destructive cycles
  • You seek deeper self-awareness

BRAINSPOTTING

Brainspotting is a powerful therapeutic tool that works with the deep brain and body to access and heal trauma and emotional pain. This approach helps you process difficult experiences and emotions that may be stored in your body and nervous system.

YOU MAY BENEFIT FROM THIS APPROACH IF:

  • You've experienced trauma or PTSD
  • You have anxiety or depression
  • You struggle with performance issues
  • You have unexplained emotional reactions
  • You feel stuck in therapy
  • You want to process deep emotions
❀
❋

EMDR

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) is a scientifically proven approach for treating trauma and other distressing life experiences. This therapy helps your brain process difficult memories and emotions in a way that leads to deep healing and resolution.

YOU MAY BENEFIT FROM THIS APPROACH IF:

  • You've experienced trauma or PTSD
  • You have recurring nightmares
  • You struggle with anxiety
  • You have phobias or fears
  • You experience panic attacks
  • You have negative self-beliefs

POLYVAGAL THEORY

Polyvagal Theory provides a framework for understanding how our nervous system responds to safety and threat. This approach helps you develop greater awareness of your body's signals and learn to regulate your nervous system for better emotional well-being.

YOU MAY BENEFIT FROM THIS APPROACH IF:

  • You experience chronic stress
  • You have difficulty with emotions
  • You struggle with relationships
  • You have trauma responses
  • You experience anxiety
  • You want better emotional regulation

✿

CLINICAL ART THERAPY

Clinical Art Therapy combines traditional psychotherapy with creative expression, offering a unique way to explore emotions and experiences. This approach can help you access and process feelings that may be difficult to express through words alone.

YOU MAY BENEFIT FROM THIS APPROACH IF:

  • You struggle to express emotions
  • You process visually or creatively
  • You want to explore self-expression
  • You have trauma or anxiety
  • You seek self-discovery
  • You want alternative healing methods
