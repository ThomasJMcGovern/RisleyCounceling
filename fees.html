INSURANCE & REIMBURSEMENT

Out-of-Network Benefits

While I do not directly accept insurance, many clients use their out-of-network benefits for reimbursement. I provide detailed superbills that include all necessary information for insurance claims.

I recommend contacting your insurance provider to verify your out-of-network mental health benefits.

Questions to Ask Your Insurance

  • What are my out-of-network mental health benefits?
  • What is my deductible and has it been met?
  • What percentage of fees are reimbursed?
  • Is there a limit on sessions per year?
  • Do I need pre-authorization?

Superbill Services

I provide monthly superbills with all required information for insurance reimbursement, including:

  • Diagnosis codes (when clinically appropriate)
  • CPT procedure codes
  • Session dates and fees
  • Provider information and NPI number

SLIDING SCALE AVAILABILITY

I maintain a limited number of sliding scale spots for clients experiencing financial hardship. These spots are offered based on need and availability. Please inquire during our initial consultation if you need sliding scale consideration.

POLICIES & PROCEDURES

Cancellation Policy

I understand that life happens. If you need to cancel or reschedule, please provide at least 24 hours notice. Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours notice will be charged the full session fee.

Late Arrivals

Sessions begin and end at scheduled times. If you arrive late, we will still end at the regular time to respect the next client's appointment. The full fee applies regardless of session length.

Good Faith Estimate

In compliance with the No Surprises Act, I provide good faith estimates for the expected costs of treatment. This will be discussed during our initial consultation.
